About

I'm Ryan Novas, web developer and Director of Operations at Quill.org, which I cofounded in 2014.

I've taught high school English, done SEO, paid search and affiliate marketing, made grilled cheese sandwiches, and was marketing coordinator for a record setting meteorite auction. Fortunately, I realized that coding is way more fun than any of those other things.

Rails is my sweet spot, as I've been building with it the longest, but I also do plenty of front-end, mess around with D3.js, and just started using React at work.

I live in Brooklyn, and I'm always interested in new opportunities and collaborations.
